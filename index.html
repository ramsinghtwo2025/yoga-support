About Us

At SoulStretch Yoga, we believe that yoga is a journey open to everyone—regardless of age, experience, or background. Our passion is helping you discover balance, strength, and inner peace through a wide range of offerings, from playful baby and kids’ yoga to empowering Hatha, Vinyasa, and hot yoga practices. Whether you’re learning your first pose, exploring the joy of partner yoga, or deepening your spiritual path with Kundli Yog, we are here to walk beside you with support and inspiration. With our online yoga academy, you can move, breathe, and grow wherever life takes you—surrounded by soothing music, expert guidance, and a welcoming community. Join us at SoulStretch Yoga and awaken the harmony within you.
